1.         ENGAGEMENT

Client hereby engages and retains MSP to render Services and/or provide Products, as specifically set forth and limited in the "Proposal" or "Quote" provided by MSP. A Proposal or Quote becomes a binding "Statement of Work" ("SOW") only upon written or electronic signature by authorized representatives of both parties, including any subsequent executed work orders or service tickets under any SOW. Except as otherwise stated therein, subsequent SOWs shall be made a part of and subject to the terms contained in this MSA. No Product or Services will be provided as part of this Agreement without the execution of a written or electronically executed SOW or other mutually acceptable documentation, each of which must be executed by both parties hereto. Upon such execution, all terms and conditions of this MSA shall for all purposes be deemed incorporated into each SOW. In the event of any conflict between an SOW or Work Order and this MSA, the MSA will control except where an SOW expressly identifies by section number specific MSA provisions it is intended to override, provided that no SOW may override the following core terms: payment obligations (Section 3), confidentiality (Section 4), intellectual property (Section 10), limitation of liability (Sections 18 and 19), termination (Section 5), or governing law and jurisdiction (Section 26).

Client understands and acknowledges that MSP's pricing may be subject to change during the term of the Agreement. Except as and to the extent such price changes may be made on other terms and conditions contained in Client's SOW, MSP will provide Client with thirty (30) days' prior written notification of such price changes. Client may terminate the affected SOW upon written notice if Client does not accept the price changes, with such termination effective as of the date the price changes would otherwise take effect.
